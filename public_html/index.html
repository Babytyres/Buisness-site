Baby Tyre Retraders

Our retreading policy is simple, To maximise tyre life and reduce running costs for our customers. Retreading makes financial sense and is an integral part of good tyre management.

Tyre Retrading

Retreading is a tyre reconditioning process, which extends the economic life of tyres and is an important part of our business. As the tyre casing has been produced to be capable of more than one life, to use this potential by replacing the worn tread makes sound environmental and economic sense.
